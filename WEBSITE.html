The Process

From sound to meaning

01

Capture & Isolate

Dual mic array captures speech up to 6m. AI noise-cancellation strips ambient sound and isolates the target voice.

02

Recognize & Parse

Onboard neural chip detects the source language instantly. Context-aware parsing captures meaning, not just words.

03

Translate & Deliver

Translation is spoken through bone conduction audio in sync with the speaker's cadence — natural and immediate.

04
